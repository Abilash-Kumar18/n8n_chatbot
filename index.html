About Our Department

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in computer science education and research, producing professionals who can drive innovation and solve real-world challenges.

Our Mission

To provide a robust curriculum that blends theoretical foundations with practical application, to foster a vibrant research environment, and to equip students with the ethical and professional skills needed for a successful career.

State-of-the-Art Labs

Gain hands-on experience in our specialized labs: AI & Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, Data Science, IoT & Embedded Systems, and a High-Performance Computing Center.

Academic Programs

B.E in CSE

Our 4-year undergraduate program provides a strong foundation in computer science with options to specialize in AI & ML, Cybersecurity, and Data Science. The curriculum is designed to produce industry-ready graduates.

M.E in CSE

A 2-year postgraduate program focused on advanced topics and research. Students engage in cutting-edge projects under the guidance of expert faculty, preparing them for specialized roles and further studies.

Ph.D. Program

Our doctoral program is for aspiring researchers who wish to contribute to the body of knowledge in computer science. We offer a supportive environment for high-impact research.
